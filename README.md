# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Tools Used
- MySQL (data storage & analysis)
- Looker Studio (dashboard & visualization)

## Dataset
9,800 rows of retail sales data across 4 years

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which region generates highest sales?
2. Which product category sells most?
3. Which customer segment brings most revenue?
4. Top 10 cities by sales
5. Most popular shipping mode
6. Monthly sales trend
7. Top 10 best selling products
8. Top 10 states by revenue

## Key Findings
- West region contributes 31.4% of total sales
- Technology is the top selling category
- Consumer segment dominates with 52% of orders
- New York City is the #1 city by sales
- Total Revenue Analysed: $2,261,536.97
